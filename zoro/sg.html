Safety Guidelines and Emergency Numbers

Safety Guidelines

Here are some essential safety guidelines to follow in case of an emergency:

  • Always stay calm and assess the situation carefully.
  • If you're in a building, evacuate immediately if there is a fire.
  • If you're on the road, keep your seatbelt on and follow traffic rules.
  • Make sure to stay in touch with loved ones, especially in crisis situations.
  • Know where the nearest hospital or emergency service centers are located.
  • If you're injured, avoid moving unless it is necessary to do so.
  • Follow the instructions given by emergency responders and stay alert.
